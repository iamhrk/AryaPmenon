Something About Me..

Hi, I am Arya P Menon. I'm a devoted practitioner of Nangyarkoothu and Ottanthullal as well as a classical dancer from Kerala. I am currently pursuing my Bachelor's degree in computer science and engineering from Kerala Technological University. I have been active as a performer of these traditional artforms since a young age. I aspire to explore the depths of traditions and in addition, to find other than the definite pattern and spaces for my art.
